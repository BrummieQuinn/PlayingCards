Four of a kind

Inspired by the work of Selfthinker - HTML and CSS playing cards

Instructions
Desktop: Hover cursor over cards to raise.
Click once to expand - click again to decrease size of card.
Click and hold to flip card - release to return to original position.

Mobile: Touching card will cause it to raise and expand - touch again to decrease size of card and return to orginal position.
Touch and hold to flip card - stop touching card to return to original position.


Created originally with HTML and CSS - Javascript interactivity added later.
